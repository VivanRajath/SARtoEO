1. OVERVIEW
------------------------------------------------------------

Task:
Given a Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) image, generate the
corresponding Electro-Optical (EO/RGB) image.

Objective:
Evaluate the candidate's ability to:
- Understand SAR and EO data
- Design and implement a solution
- Justify technical decisions
- Analyze results critically
- Communicate findings effectively

Important:
SAR-to-EO translation is an ill-posed problem because SAR
contains no direct color information. Multiple EO images
may correspond to the same SAR image.

A well-reasoned approach is valued more than high scores
without explanation.

------------------------------------------------------------

Only the following datasets may be used:

1. SEN1-2
   - 282,384 paired patches
   - Sentinel-1 VV SAR
   - Sentinel-2 RGB

   - Size: ~44GB

2. SEN12MS
   - 180,662 triplets
   - Sentinel-1 VV+VH SAR
   - Sentinel-2 13-band imagery
   - MODIS land cover
   - Resolution: 256×256
   - Size: ~510GB

3. Sentinel-1 & Sentinel-2 Image Pairs (Kaggle)
   - Terrain-separated SAR/EO pairs

Rules:
- No external remote sensing datasets allowed.
- You may use a subset of the datasets.

- Cite all datasets used.
